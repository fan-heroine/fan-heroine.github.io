HERITAGE HEROINE:

DRINK and have FAN!

For Augmented Architectural Narratives

HERITAGE HEROINE is a fictional manga character who sponsors a new drink named FAN (For Augmented / Architectural Narratives).

FAN is a magical and refreshing liquid that when ingested will allow you to acquire heroic powers to have access to the intangible architectural heritage of the Embassy of Spain in Brussels through sound stories from the past, present and future.

The project highlights the tactile, experiential and emotional capacity of architecture beyond the built material, thereby rescuing the intangible capacity of the built architectural heritage. On the other hand, the project plays with the need that humans have to expand to inhabit, as a form of consumption, other avatars, bodies and identities.

The project has been developed by the architect Irma Arribas in collaboration with Enrique Villamuelas from RACA. RACA is an open radio space that belongs to the HYPERMEDIA research group from ETSAM - Higher Technical School of Architecture of Madrid, Spain.
